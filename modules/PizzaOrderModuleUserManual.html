Pizza Order Module - User Manual

The Pizza Order Module allows you to simulate the process of ordering a pizza step by step. Below are the instructions for using each feature:

  1. Place Order: Click Place Order to start a new pizza order.
  2. Confirm Order: After placing an order, click Confirm Order to confirm it. A confirmation message will appear.
  3. Check Inventory: Click Check Inventory to verify if all ingredients are available.
  4. Prepare Pizza: Click Prepare Pizza to simulate the pizza preparation process.
  5. Pack Pizza: Click Pack Pizza to pack the prepared pizza for delivery.
  6. Deliver Pizza: Click Deliver Pizza to simulate delivering the pizza to the customer.
  7. Confirm Delivery: Click Confirm Delivery to confirm that the pizza has been delivered.
  8. Send Failure Notice: If any step fails, click Send Failure Notice to notify the customer.

The current status of the process is shown at the bottom of the module window.
